# ENGINEERING THROUGH TIME

## DESIGN A WEBSITE PEOPLE REMEMBER

Create a premium cinematic web experience for Sanket Bhor.

This is not a portfolio.

This is not a resume.

This is not a developer website.

This is a story.

A story about how an engineer evolves.

A story about how curiosity becomes expertise.

How expertise becomes systems.

How systems become leverage.

How leverage becomes impact.

The visitor should feel like they are watching a beautifully directed documentary rather than browsing a website.

The website should feel unforgettable.

Not because of animations.

Not because of technology.

Because of the story.

---

# THE EMOTIONAL GOAL

When someone leaves the website, they should not remember:

Angular.

Accessibility.

Performance.

Developer Tooling.

Design Systems.

Instead they should remember:

"This person thinks differently."

"This person builds things that last."

"This person turns complexity into clarity."

"This person makes other people better."

"This person creates impact that compounds over time."

---

# THE EXPERIENCE

The website should unfold like a documentary told in chapters.

Every chapter represents a transformation.

Every transformation represents a shift in thinking.

Every shift in thinking reveals a deeper level of engineering maturity.

This should feel closer to:

* an A24 documentary
* a Netflix original series
* an Apple launch story
* a museum exhibit
* a beautifully designed biography

than a traditional personal website.

---

# VISUAL LANGUAGE

The visual design should feel timeless.

Not trendy.

Not startup-like.

Not futuristic.

Not AI generated.

Imagine:

A luxury coffee table book.

A Leica photography publication.

An architectural journal.

A museum exhibition catalogue.

A premium documentary title sequence.

---

# COLOR SYSTEM

Use a restrained monochrome palette.

Background:
#0B0B0B

Surface:
#141414

Primary Text:
#F5F5F5

Muted Text:
#8B8B8B

Borders:
#232323

Accent:
#D7D2C8

No bright blue.

No cyan.

No purple.

No gradients.

No neon.

No glowing effects.

No glassmorphism.

No SaaS aesthetics.

The design should feel sophisticated and cinematic.

---

# TYPOGRAPHY

Typography carries the experience.

Typography is the hero.

Use extremely large editorial typography.

Generous whitespace.

Strong hierarchy.

Confident layouts.

Minimal decorative elements.

The words themselves should create emotion.

---

Massive whitespace.

No navigation initially.

No cards.

No metrics.

No logos.

No social icons.

Then:

ENGINEERING THROUGH TIME

A documentary in six chapters.

Subtle invitation:

Begin

The visitor should immediately feel curiosity.
